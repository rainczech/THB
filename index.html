"worry about the more important things"

You're engaged! Congratulations! Now begins the most busy and exciting time of your life as you put together your wedding.


The Honorary Bridesmaid stemmed from the concept that picking out and designing invitations is fun, but sitting for hours to put them together, and then driving to the post office is not. Having been a bridesmaid 7 times (and counting?), I understand the stress that both brides and bridesmaids go through with making everything come together for the special day.


We'll do the work, so you can worry about the more important things.

what exactly do we do?

We are a full-service stationery assistant; we take care of all the details. We will pick up your invitations from either you or your stationer, and we'll take care of the rest. Services include....



  • doneAssembling your invitations - this also includes attaching/tying ribbons.

  • doneStuffing your assembled invitations in their envelopes.

  • doneAddressing the envelopes to your guests
  • doneSealing your envelopes with our logo for that professional touch

  • doneGoing to the post office to get the correct postage.

  • doneMailing your invitations for you!

frequently asked questions

Have some questions? Here are some answers...

is this just for weddings and invitations?

Absolutely not! We help with all occasions such as baby shower invitations, and yearly Christmas cards.

how much do you charge?

The long and convoluted answer is that the price depends on the amount of labor and postage required. A simple invitation requires less labor and postage due to less materials. On the other hand, a more elaborate invitation requires more work such as assembling layers and attaching ribbons. Plus postage will cost higher due to the extra weight.


The short answer is for a "basic" full service option where we: pick up your invitations, stuff, address,stamp, and mail them for you, starts at $1.25 and up not including postage. So expect about $1.75 and up per invite.

do I have to be a bride to use you?

Nope. We are here to help. If you are a bridesmaid and are busy yourself with your own job and other responsibilities, we can send your bridal shower invites for you.
